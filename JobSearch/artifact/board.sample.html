Job / Dispatch

Sync now applies these automatically (needs the Claude app open, and a Drive tool configured — see README). Copy for chat is the manual fallback and always works.

This board is a snapshot of jobs.json embedded at build time — “Refresh board data” rebuilds it, “Run research now” hunts for new jobs (both need the Claude app open; reload the artifact afterwards). Marking a job “I'm interested” auto-drafts a tailored resume + cover letter on the next Sync (previews show up here — real files live in your Applications folder). Scheduling an interview round moves a job to Interviewing and queues auto-generated study notes; “Talk about this” copies full job/interview context to paste into a new chat message. Submission always happens in chat with your explicit approval.
