## Known gaps / open questions (fill via daily questions)
Anything you know is incomplete, uncertain, or not yet decided. The research and drafting

## Standing application policies
Decisions you don't want re-litigated on every single application — write the answer once,
here, and every future application uses it automatically. Common ones to define:
- **Education:** whether/how to disclose it, and what to select if a form forces a choice.
- **Work authorization:** citizenship/visa status, sponsorship needs, notice period.
- **Voluntary self-identification questions** (gender, race, ethnicity, disability, veteran
  status): your standing answer (e.g. "always decline/prefer not to answer").
- Anything else you find yourself answering the same way, application after application.
