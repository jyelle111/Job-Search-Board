## What to find
- What counts as "in scope"? (full-time / part-time / contract / consulting / any
  combination — be as broad or narrow as you want)

  search pass per family, so more families = broader coverage but longer runs):

  2. [Role family 2, e.g. "Staff/Principal Technical Product Manager"]
  3. [Role family 3]

## Compensation floors
- Full-time: **≥ $X/yr base**
- Contract/consulting: **≥ $X/hr**
- Always include, per match: market range for the role + an estimate of what this specific
  company likely pays (funding stage, size, prior postings).

## Location
- Home base: [City, State / metro]
- Local (onsite/hybrid range): [list the metro areas you'll commute to]
- Remote: [anywhere in the US? specific countries? not open to remote?]
- Willing to relocate: [Yes/No] — the auto-fill pipeline defaults every "willing to
  relocate?" screening question to this answer unless you say otherwise for a specific role.

## Process rules (hard requirements)
1. Never resurface a job you've already passed/skipped.
2. Never submit an application without your explicit approval of (a) the decision to apply
   and (b) the final documents.
3. Every match must include: fit score, why-good-fit, why-not-fit, market salary range,
   likely company pay.
4. Ask questions whenever information is missing (application form fields, profile gaps) —
   never guess.
5. [Add your own resume/cover-letter style preference here, e.g. "ATS-safe single column,
   one page unless history genuinely doesn't fit."]
6. Max 1–3 profile-gap questions per check-in; log answers back to `master-profile.md`.
